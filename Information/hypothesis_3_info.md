# Hypothesis 3 — Dispute risk and loss drivers

## 1. Executive summary

- Overall dispute rate is 2.73% and disputed amount share is 2.89%.
- Dispute risk is higher in higher-risk merchant tiers and in specific categories such as Travel, Jewelry, Hotel, and Gaming.
- The largest financial losses come from fraud and product-not-received disputes.
- A small set of merchants shows very high dispute intensity and should be treated as operational hotspots.

## 8. Key takeaways

1. Dispute loss is not random; it is concentrated in a few categories and causes.
2. Fraud and product-not-received issues are the most damaging in value terms.
3. Travel and Jewelry generate the highest absolute disputed amounts.
4. Merchant monitoring should focus on high-risk categories and high-loss merchants first.
5. Operational response should combine prevention, dispute handling, and stronger monitoring of high-risk accounts.
